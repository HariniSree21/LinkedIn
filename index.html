What is linkedin?

LinkedIn is a professional networking platform where individuals create profiles to showcase their professional experience, skills, and achievements. Students can use LinkedIn to connect with professionals, join industry-related groups, and discover job or internship opportunities. It helps them build a professional online presence and network with potential mentors or employers.

Why LinkedIn?

        
LinkedIn helps students develop various skills, including:

1. *Networking Skills:* Students can connect with professionals, alumni, and peers, expanding their professional network.

2. *Communication Skills:* Through interactions, messaging, and posting content, students can enhance their written communication skills.

3. *Profile Optimization:* Crafting a compelling LinkedIn profile helps students effectively present their skills, experiences, and achievements.

4. *Research Skills:* Students can explore industries, companies, and career paths, gaining valuable insights for informed decision-making.

5. *Professional Branding:* Creating a strong personal brand on LinkedIn showcases a student's strengths and interests to potential employers.

6. *Job Search Skills:* Using LinkedIn's job search feature helps students identify job opportunities, and they can learn about job requirements and industry trends.

7. *Self-Promotion:* Sharing projects, achievements, or insights allows students to practice promoting themselves in a professional manner.
